Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before registering.

Visit the Registration page, fill in your details — name, roll number, branch, email, mobile — and select the event you wish to attend. Submit the form and you'll receive a confirmation email.

Most events are free of cost for enrolled students. The Hackathon has a nominal team registration fee of ₹100 per team. All fees will be collected at the venue on the day of the event.

Yes! You can register for multiple events as long as there is no schedule conflict. Each event registration must be submitted separately through the registration form.

Carry your college ID card and the confirmation email you receive after registration. For technical events, personal laptops are recommended but not mandatory — lab systems will be available.

Currently, events are open to students of our college only. Inter-college participation may be announced for select events — watch the notice board and this website for updates.
